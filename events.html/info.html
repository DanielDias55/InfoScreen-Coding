List of Rules:

  • No smoking inside rooms or common areas.

  • Respect quiet hours, typically during the evening and night.

  • Keep noise level down to ensure a conductive living environment.

  • Maintain cleanliness and tidiness in both individual and common areas.

  • Dispose the trash properly and follow the recycling guidelines.

  • Respect the privacy and personal space of fellow residents.

  • Follow fire safety regulations including not tampering with smoke detectors and fire alarms.

  • Use appliances responsibly and follow any restrictions on high energy appliances.

  • Do not engage in any illegal activities on the premises.

  • No pets allowed in the dormitory.

  • Do not make alterations to the room and common areas without prior approval.

  • Abide by the specific rules regarding the use of shared kitchens and facilities.

  • Must respect and follow the cleaning duties schedule and perform accordingly if you don’t want to get fined by the management.

  • Be considerate of cultural and personal differences among fellow residents.

  • Adhere to any specific rules related to parking or bicycle storage.
